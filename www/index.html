Good Touch vs Bad Touch

Safe touch feels comfortable, like a handshake. If any touch makes you feel scared, confused, or sad, say NO immediately and tell parents.

School Bus Safety

Always sit properly in the bus. Do not poke your head or hands out of windows, and only board or alight after the bus stops fully.

🚫

Stranger Danger

Never accept chocolates, gifts, or rides from people you do not know. Keep safe distance and run to a crowd if followed.

Self Defense Actions

Aim for sensitive points (eyes, throat, groin) if physically assaulted. Use keys or phone as improvised defense tools.

Safe Solo Travel

Cyber Safety & Stalking
